## Approval Email Template

Send this within 60 seconds of receiving a valid request.
Copy, fill the two brackets, send.

---

Subject: Big Brain Access — Approved

Hi [NAME],

You're approved. Download the files from the repo and follow the install
instructions for your platform in README.md.

A few things before you start:

1. Your approval is individual. Don't share access with teammates — each
   person on your team needs their own approval. Team licenses are available
   at a flat rate (see enterprise-license.md in the repo).

2. Attribution is required. Any AI output produced while Big Brain is active
   should include: "Powered by Big Brain — Polaris Digital Studio."

3. At the 30-day mark I'll send one question. Takes 10 seconds to answer.
   That feedback is how the routing logic gets better over time.

4. If you want to bring in a colleague, nominate them using the template below
   and they'll get a faster approval path.

Questions: reply to this email.

— Joseph
polarisdgtl.com

## Rejection Template (for requests that don't qualify)

Subject: Big Brain Access — Not Right Now

Hi [NAME],

Thanks for the request. I'm not opening access right now for [reason:
commercial use without license / incomplete request / use case doesn't fit].

If your situation changes, send another request with more detail about
what you're building and how you plan to use it.

— Joseph

## 30-Day Feedback Email (send to each approved user at 30 days)

Subject: Quick question — Big Brain at 30 days

Hi [NAME],

One question, 10 seconds:

Did Big Brain change a decision you were going to make? If yes, what was the
decision and what did the review surface that you hadn't considered?

No wrong answer. This is how the routing logic gets smarter for the next
person who uses it.

— Joseph
